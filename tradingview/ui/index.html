        Step 1 of 4 · Welcome

        One run per day.

        1runOS is your second pair of eyes on the chart. Each day it reads your setup, gives you a thesis, journals what happens, and remembers the lesson for tomorrow.

        • ① Capture the chart you're watching.
        • ② Analyze — Claude reads it, returns entry / stop / TP.
        • ③ Trade the thesis — order entry is one click away.
        • ④ Learn — the journal feeds tomorrow's read.
        Step 2 of 4 · Chrome

        Connect to your Chrome.

        1runOS drives your real Chrome to read TradingView and talk to Claude. No login details stored here — your existing sessions stay in your browser.

        Checking Chrome on port 9222…
        Chrome isn't running with remote debugging. Paste this into Terminal:
        cd ~/Desktop/IntelligenceOS/tradingview && ./start_chrome_cdp.sh
        Then sign into tradingview.com and claude.ai once. Click Re-check below.
